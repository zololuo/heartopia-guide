Beginner Guide

Getting Started

The 3 most important decisions in your first 30 minutes — avoid all beginner traps

🎯 Three Critical Early Choices

DecisionBest PickWhy
3rd HobbyCooking 🍳Most profitable early game — raw blueberries sell for 16g; 4 blueberries as jam sell for 170g
First to UpgradeGardening → Lv.2 & Fishing → Lv.2Gardening Lv.2 boosts crop value; Fishing Lv.2 increases high-star fish chance
DG Level GoalLv.4 (Multiplayer) → Lv.14 (Painting)Lv.4 unlocks multiplayer; Lv.14 unlocks custom painting feature

🚀 Optimal First 3 Days

📅 Day 1 — Rush main quest until fishing + gardening unlocked → plant first blueberries → cook jam to build starting capital
📅 Day 2 — Push DG to Lv.3 → start doing Resident Requests → sleep in bed overnight to restore stamina
📅 Day 3 — Rush DG Lv.4 to unlock multiplayer → upgrade your tools (axe, fishing rod) first

⚠️ Four Beginner Traps to Avoid

❌ Never cook high-star fish! 3★ fish sell for 150-400g directly. Only use low-star fish for cooking.
❌ Don't build a huge house early! Room size should be 6×6 or 8×8 tiles — larger rooms limit furniture placement.
✅ Join the DG Guild immediately! The entire game's progression system is locked behind DG level.
✅ Use the bus to travel! Much faster than walking — saves tons of time.

🍳 Jam Cooking Strategy

  1. Get Gardening to Lv.2
  2. Mass-plant blueberries (cheap, fast growth)
  3. 4 blueberries → 1 Blueberry Jam
  4. Sell jam to Albert Jr.
  5. Net profit: 106g per jar
💡 Batch workflow: fill field → harvest all → cook all → sell all at once

🎣 Fishing Profit Strategy

  1. Get Fishing to Lv.2
  2. Prioritize sea fishing (more XP)
  3. 3★ fish → sell directly (150-400g)
  4. 1-2★ fish → cook into dishes, then sell
  5. Use bait in your home pond to grind fish
⚠️ Never cook a 3★ fish worth 300+g — you'll lose most of its value!

💡 Optimal Stamina Strategy

☀️ Daytime: Spend stamina on high-value activities (Fishing > Woodcutting > Mining). Eat food when low.
🌙 Night: Always return to your bed and sleep before logging off — wake up with full stamina tomorrow.
⚠️ Don't waste: Gathering berries, mushrooms, and harvesting crops cost zero stamina — do these anytime, not when stamina is full.

The Resident Request Office is NOT a physical building — it's a menu accessed via the button next to the map. Complete one Weekly Request to finish this quest.

NPC Gifting Guide

Every villager's loved & hated gifts, birthday, and whereabouts — build friendships faster

🎁 How Gifting Works

Give gifts up to 2× per week per NPC. Loved gifts give the most friendship points (♥♥♥). Neutral gifts are ok but slow. Hated gifts reduce friendship — avoid them!
💡 Pro tip: Give a loved gift on an NPC's birthday for 3× friendship points. Blueberry Jam is a universally liked gift for almost everyone.

Crop Calendar

Season-by-season planting guide — what to plant, when to harvest, and max profit per day

📅 Planting Strategy

🥔 Potatoes are king: Across all seasons, potatoes consistently deliver the best profit/day ratio. Plant them whenever they're in season.
🫐 Blueberries pay twice: Don't just sell raw blueberries — cook 4 into Blueberry Jam for 165% profit. Dedicate at least half your field to blueberries in every season they're available.

Tool Upgrades

Upgrade costs, materials, and benefits for every tool — know what to upgrade first

🔨 Upgrade Priority & Strategy

🪓 Axe first → 🎣 Fishing Rod second → ⛏️ Pickaxe third → 💧 Watering Can fourth → 🔧 Hoe last. Axe unlocks better wood for crafting; Fishing Rod improves catch quality; Hoe is lowest priority since tilling soil is a one-time action.
💰 Budget tip: Each upgrade costs increasingly more. Don't upgrade all tools evenly — max out your Axe and Fishing Rod before touching the others.
